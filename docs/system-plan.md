# Mashego Professional Steel and Woodworks System Plan

## Business Scope
Mashego Professional Steel and Woodworks provides steel fabrication and woodworking services.

## Main Services
- Gates
- Burglar bars
- Carports
- Steel structures
- Custom steel fabrication
- Staircases
- Balustrades
- Furniture
- Cabinets
- Wardrobes
- Shelving
- Custom woodwork projects

## System Goal
The system will help the company manage:
- Customer requests
- Quotations
- Site measurements
- Job tracking
- Workshop production
- Installation
- Invoicing
- Customer communication
- Reporting

## Main Users
- Customers
- Sales / Estimators
- Workshop Staff
- Installation Teams
- Admin / Managers

## Core Modules
1. Customer Management
2. Enquiries / Leads
3. Measurements
4. Quotations
5. Job Cards
6. Workshop Management
7. Inventory
8. Quality Control
9. Installation Tracking
10. Invoicing
11. Reports
12. Support / Warranty

## System Outcome
The system should improve:
- Professionalism
- Efficiency
- Record keeping
- Job tracking
- Customer service
- Internal control
