# Business Workflow

## Step 1: Customer Enquiry
The customer submits a request for a steel or woodworking project.

## Step 2: Capture Details
Staff records:
- Customer information
- Project description
- Photos or drawings
- Location
- Approximate dimensions
- Material preference

## Step 3: Site Measurement
If needed, a site visit is done and exact measurements are captured.

## Step 4: Quote Creation
A quotation is prepared with:
- Materials
- Labour
- Finishing
- Delivery
- Installation
- Terms and conditions

## Step 5: Customer Approval
The customer approves the quotation and pays a deposit if required.

## Step 6: Job Card Creation
A job card is generated for production and installation.

## Step 7: Workshop Production
The workshop team fabricates the steel or woodwork item.

## Step 8: Quality Control
The completed work is checked for:
- Accuracy
- Finish
- Strength
- Fitment

## Step 9: Installation
The item is installed on site and customer sign-off is captured.

## Step 10: Invoice and Closeout
The final invoice is issued and the job is marked complete.
