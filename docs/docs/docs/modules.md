# System Modules

## 1. Customer Management
Stores customer profiles and job history.

## 2. Enquiries / Leads
Tracks new customer requests and project interest.

## 3. Measurements
Stores site visit information, dimensions, and photos.

## 4. Quotations
Creates and manages estimates for steel and woodwork jobs.

## 5. Job Cards
Tracks approved work from start to completion.

## 6. Workshop Management
Manages fabrication and woodworking tasks.

## 7. Inventory
Tracks steel, timber, fittings, fasteners, paint, and consumables.

## 8. Quality Control
Checks workmanship before delivery or installation.

## 9. Installation
Tracks on-site installation activity and completion.

## 10. Invoicing
Handles deposits, balances, and final billing.

## 11. Reports
Provides summaries of jobs, quotes, payments, and performance.

## 12. Support / Warranty
Handles follow-up service, complaints, and warranty requests.
